Community Tech Workshop: Building Liberation Technology

Date: February 20, 2025

Time: 18:00 - 21:00

Location: Community Center, South London

Organizer: BLKOUT Tech Collective

Join us for an empowering workshop where community members will learn to build and maintain their own digital infrastructure. This hands-on session covers:

  • Setting up secure communications
  • Building community websites
  • Digital security for activists
  • Collective ownership of technology

This workshop is designed for all skill levels, from complete beginners to experienced developers. We believe in liberation through technological literacy and community empowerment.

Capacity: 25 people

Cost: Free / Pay-what-you-can

Contact: tech@blkout.org
